# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors should keep discussions respectful, technical, and evidence-driven. Assume good intent, but be precise when reviewing code that can affect platform state, credentials, or research results.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Posting another user's credentials, private alpha IDs, run outputs, or account-specific data.
- Encouraging users to violate platform terms or hide live submission behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that put users' credentials, accounts, or private research data at risk.
